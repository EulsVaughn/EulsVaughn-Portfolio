Practicum Overview

For my practicum, I chose the host-training establishment at Dark League Studios, focusing on learning paths that include Project Management (PM) and its fundamentals, Data Analytics, Business Process Improvement, and Workplace Management. In my specializations, I focused on honing skills in PM, Business Process Improvement, and communication.

Throughout my practicum at Dark League Studios, these learning paths have proven invaluable. Engaging in tasks such as coordinating events, managing client communications, and conducting data analysis underscored the practical application of PM and data analytics. These experiences reinforced the importance of structured project planning, effective stakeholder communication, and data-driven decision-making in achieving project goals. Moreover, as an esports project manager intern, I applied these principles daily, overseeing tournament logistics, managing participant engagement, and ensuring smooth event execution. These experiences have provided a direct application of PM principles in a dynamic, real-world setting, preparing me to leverage these fundamentals in Information Technology PM roles, Business Analyst and IT Auditor in the future.

In my specialized areas, the focus on Business Process Improvement enabled me to identify and implement operational efficiencies, improving workflow and project outcomes. Enhanced communication skills facilitated effective team collaboration and client engagement, critical in managing relationships and achieving project success in a fast-paced environment like Dark League Studios.

My practicum required a total of 324 hours, aligning with the curriculum expectations of an Information Systems student. These immersive experiences not only enhanced my hard skills in web development and data analytics but also sharpened my soft skills in teamwork, communication, and project management. They have prepared me effectively for the demands of the IT industry, ensuring I am equipped to contribute effectively and innovate in future professional endeavors.
